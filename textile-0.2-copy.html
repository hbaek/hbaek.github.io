Sonic Play ground

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

2014

Proecessing and Watercolor

 

Sonic Playground was exhibited at the MCA (Museum of Contemporary Art of Chicago) and was a collaborative project between two classes lead by  Lindsey French and Eric Lenordson. Three domestic locations in the loop were monitored and broadcast at the MCA. The sketch responded to the environmental acoustics and draws a vibration of rings.
