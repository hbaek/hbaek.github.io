Sonic  Playground

 

 

 

 

2014

 

Proecessing and Watercolor

 

 

 

Sonic Playground was exhibited at the MCA (Museum of Contemporary Art of Chicago) and was a collaborative project between two classes lead by  Lindsey French and Eric Lenordson at SAIC.

 

Three domestic locations in the loop were monitored and broadcasted at the MCA. The sketch responded to the environmental acoustics and draws a vibration of rings.

Under The Willow Tree

 

 

 

 

 

2014

Buckram, Millinery and Floral Wire, Yarn, Deco Mesh

 

 

 

 

 

Drawn to Park NoSoo's watercolor folding screen called 'Under the willows'(柳下), I created an installation piece that the audience can engage in both from the distance and underneath it.  The Installation was lowered down to the audience's height level.
