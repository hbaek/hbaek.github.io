art & technology |  Sonic Playground at the MCA

Sonic Playground was exhibited at the MCA (Museum of Contemporary Art of Chicago) and was collaborative project between two classes lead by  Lindsey French and Eric Lenordson. Three locations are placed with a live broadcasting mic where you can hear the train pass by once a while, the typing sounds of the laptop and peoples chattering in the street. The white circles indicates the volume of the sound and location of the mics.
